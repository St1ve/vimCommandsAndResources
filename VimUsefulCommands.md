# Hotkeys: 
### Switching modes: 
- 'i' - insert mode
- 'esc' - "movement" mode
- 'v' - visual mode 
### Move commands:
- h
- j
- k
- 'l
### Undo/Redo: 
- 'u' or 'g-' - undo
- '' or  'g+' - redo
### Copy and paste:
- select text in visual mode, then press 'y' to copy selection and press 'p' to paste.
